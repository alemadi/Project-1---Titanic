Conclusion:¶

According to my initial hypothesis.

Findings that matched the hypothesis:

  • Young adults are the most likely to survive (20-30 years old)
  • Upper classes had a higher survival rate

Findings that did not match with my initial hypothesis:

  • In fact, females had a better survival rate, not males.

Final Thoughts on the Dataset:

There were 1,317 passengers in the titanic (http://www.titanicfacts.net/). That means there are 426 missing passengers from the dataset. Although there 177 rows missing from the age column, I do not think it affected the findings of analysis much. I was also interested in the survival rates from each cabin, that would give us an interesting insight of which parts of the ship had a higher survival rate, unfortunately, most of that column is missing (687 values).
